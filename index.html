Laboratory Management System

Customizing Dolibarr ERP for Medical Laboratories This project focuses on the customization of Dolibarr ERP, an open-source enterprise resource planning system, tailored for medical laboratory management. Dolibarr‚Äôs modular structure allows for seamless adaptation to meet the unique requirements of healthcare environments, particularly in managing lab workflows, patient records, and report generation. Key custom modules include:

Dolibarr ERP PostgreSQL Custom Modules
Gross Module:
  • Automates the documentation of tissue sample examinations.
  • Allows lab technicians to record size, shape, and other characteristics with image integration.
  • Ensures data is accurately captured and linked to patient records.
Transcription Module:
  • Integrates voice recognition to transcribe verbal findings from lab technicians and doctors.
  • Minimizes errors and speeds up documentation.
Doctor Module:
  • Provides doctors with streamlined access to lab results, patient records, and diagnostic tools.
  • Enables efficient collaboration with lab personnel and allows requesting additional tests through the system.
Automated Report Generation Module:
  • Compiles data from other modules (e.g., gross examinations, transcription) into cohesive reports.
  • Uses predefined templates to reduce manual reporting efforts and ensure consistency.
Histopath Lab Module:
  • Manages the examination of tissue samples under a microscope.
  • Integrates with other modules to streamline the diagnostic process for diseases like cancer.
  • Allows image attachments for more comprehensive reporting.
Cytopath Lab Module:
  • Focuses on cell-level analysis for diagnosing diseases such as cancers and infections.
  • Integrates with patient records and allows high-resolution image storage for accurate cell analysis.
